About the Event

Where Innovation Meets Collaboration

DevCon 2025 is a premier technology and innovation festival that brings together developers, tech enthusiasts, and industry leaders under one roof. It’s a dynamic platform to showcase cutting-edge technologies, attend insightful sessions, and participate in exciting challenges.

The event is designed to inspire creativity, foster networking, and provide hands-on learning opportunities in the rapidly evolving world of technology. Whether you’re a student, professional, or startup enthusiast, this conference offers the perfect environment to learn, share, and grow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are student discounts available?

Yes, we offer a Student Pass at a significantly reduced price. You must present a valid student ID at check-in.

What is the refund policy?

Tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person up to 7 days before the event starts.

Do I need to register for workshops separately?

If you have an All Access or Corporate Pass, workshop entry is guaranteed. Student Pass holders can enter via a lottery system one week before the event.
